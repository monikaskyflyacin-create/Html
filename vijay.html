Thalapathy Vijay, born Joseph Vijay Chandrasekhar on 22 June 1974 in Chennai, is one of the most celebrated actors in Tamil cinema. Known for his charismatic screen presence, energetic dance moves, and mass appeal, Vijay has acted in over 65 films since his debut in 1992. Over the years, he has delivered numerous blockbuster hits like Ghilli, Thuppakki, Mersal, Master, and Leo, which earned him a massive fan following across the globe. Apart from acting, he is also a playback singer and philanthropist, often supporting education and welfare initiatives through his fan clubs. In 2024, Vijay entered politics by launching his party, Tamilaga Vettri Kazhagam (TVK), with a vision of creating transparent governance and empowering the youth.
